Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Empower LO LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Broker Toolkit platform (brokertoolkit.app). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and account credentials
  • Billing and payment information (processed securely via Stripe)
  • HighLevel integration credentials (API keys, location IDs)
  • Content you create within the platform (newsletters, loan analyses, activity logs)
  • Usage data and platform interaction metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Broker Toolkit platform
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve user experience

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Payment information is processed by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We may share information with third-party service providers that assist us in operating the platform, including:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Vercel (hosting)
  • Supabase (database)
  • OpenAI (AI content generation — no personal data is sent)

Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information at any time through the platform. To request data deletion, contact us at support@brokertoolkit.app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@brokertoolkit.app.
